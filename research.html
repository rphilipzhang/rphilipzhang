Research

My research applies AI and data science to address fundamental business operations issues under the emerging trends in technology, marketplaces, and society. I am particularly enthusiastic about developing data science methodologies (machine learning, causal inference, and data-driven optimization) to evaluate and optimize the operations strategies in the contexts of online platforms and marketplaces, sharing economy, and social networks, especially their recommendation, advertising, pricing, and matching policies. I am also a strong adherent to the philosophy that business research should push the "efficient frontier" of intellectual depth and practical impact, thus striving to implement my research in business practice whenever applicable.

Research Interests

Methodologies

Machine learning and deep learning; causal inference and econometrics; reinforcement learning; data-driven optimization.

Applications

Online platforms and marketplaces; social networks; AI ethics; revenue management and pricing; inventory and supply chain management.
